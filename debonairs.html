DEBONAIRS

In 1991 during his second year of studies, MacKenzie and a colleague, Mr Andrew Harvey, who was studying for a Bachelor of Commerce degree, started Debonairs Pizza in Pietermaritzburg. The duo rented a bakery from MacKenzie's brother, Cameron, and started churning out pizzas. They were the first pizza brand in Africa to offer online & mobile ordering. Their Springsgate Drive Thru branch is the first pizza Drive Thru in Africa.

The majority of Debonairs Pizza restaurants offer Free delivery. This is indicated when you select the restaurant where you will place your order.

To give back to the community Debonairs Pizza gives consumers an opportunity to make a Doughnation. Doughnation is an initiative that Debonairs has introduced and just by ordering a Debonairs Pizza, consumers are helping to feed people in need at no additional cost to them.
